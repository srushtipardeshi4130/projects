About Us

Welcome to PizzaFactory – where passion meets pizza!

Founded in 2025, PizzaFactory started with a single mission: handcrafted pizzas to everyone. From our carefully selected ingredients to our stone-baked perfection.

Whether you're a fan of classic Margherita, spicy non-veg toppings, or cheesy garlic sides, we’ve got something to satisfy every craving. We believe in quality, freshness, and fast, friendly service.

We’re a community of food lovers who believe that good food brings people together. Join us and taste the difference!

Thank you for choosing PizzaFactory.
